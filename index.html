Kevin Haninger

  • Email: khaninger@berkeley.edu

I am a robotics and controls researcher working on control of compliant robots in manipulation and human-robot interaction

I am currently affiliated with Fraunhofer IPK in Berlin; I've previously worked at Franka Emika in Munich, Space Sciences Lab in Berkeley, Space Robotics Lab in Sendai and consulted for Gate Labs in SF.
